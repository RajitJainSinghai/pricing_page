Terms and Conditions

  • Only .jpg or .jpeg images will be delivered. .psd files, raw images, .png files will cost extra and are not part of the deliverables/prices mentioned above.
  • If raw images or .psd files are required, they will be charged extra at ₹100 +gst per item/folder.
  • If .png images are required, they will be charged extra at ₹100+gst each.
  • The delivery timeframe is 7 to 10 working days from the day the advance payment is received or the garments received, whichever occurs first.
  • Images for Amazon / Flipkart will be delivered in 2000x2000 pixels, 72 dpi, in a white backdrop as per guidelines of the respective marketplaces.
  • Images for Myntra and other fashion portals will be delivered in 1440x1080 pixels, 72 dpi, in 240 RGB, i.e. in a light grey backdrop only.
  • Higher DPI, Resolution, or Pixel sizes will incur additional costs upto ₹250 per image.
  • Extra eCommerce backdrops in white or light grey, will cost ₹100+gst per item/folder. Incase only Front image is required in white backdrop for Amazon.in, it will cost ₹25/image.
  • Any point not mentioned above, is not committed as well and may incur extra cost. Therefore, all requirements must be explicitly told and agreed upfront in writing to avoid any conflicts later.
  • We take guarantee for Go Live on marketplaces and not for styling and meeting shoot expectations as per whims and fancy of our most beloved and esteemed clients.
  • No refund will be given once shoot is done and costs incurred. Reshoot will be accepted only if it is rejected by the marketplace for not adhering to published guidelines.
  • Garments will be handed over or dispatched only when full payment is cleared for the shoot.
  • 50% advance payment is required to book the shoot, remaining 50% is required when images with watermark are delivered and okayed.
  • When we ship the garments back post shoot and there is in transit loss/theft, it won’t be refunded. To avoid this, we request you to arrange for the reverse logistics yourself.
  • Due to unavoidable circumstances there might be a change of model or unexpected delays in delivering the shoot, it will be communicated and discussed on case to case basis with you. We request your understanding and support on all such matters.
  • Minimum Shoot Quantity is Ten [10], both for Model and Product Shoot.
  • eCommerce Shoot will be done as par guidelines, for any custom guidelines, reference must be given upfront and consent taken, as there can be extra cost towards it.
